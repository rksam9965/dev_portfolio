01. About Me

I'm a Flutter developer based in Tamil Nadu, India, obsessed with building products that feel as good as they look. My path started with curiosity — and turned into a craft of shipping polished, scalable apps across every screen.

Currently at Howdy Chat Technologies, I'm building a social media hybrid that fuses Instagram-style feeds with WhatsApp messaging — implementing offline caching with SQLite & Hive, Aadhaar fingerprint auth, deep linking, and provisioning Node.js backends on AWS EC2.

Previously, I shipped apps to the App Store & Play Store at Burhani Technologies and Humblecode — working with advanced state management (BLoC, GetX), Firebase, and Django REST backends.

Outside code: chess, carrom, and pushing prompt engineering to its limits — I've even used it to create animated short films.
